About Saravali!

Saravali is a Census Town city in district of Thane, Maharashtra. The Saravali Census Town has population of 8,710 of which 5,008 are males while 3,702 are females as per report released by Census India 2011. Population of Children with age of 0-6 is 1124 which is 12.90 % of total population of Saravali (CT). In Saravali Census Town, Female Sex Ratio is of 739 against state average of 929. Moreover Child Sex Ratio in Saravali is around 834 compared to Maharashtra state average of 894. Literacy rate of Saravali city is 92.79 % higher than state average of 82.34 %. In Saravali, Male literacy is around 94.84 % while female literacy rate is 89.97 %. Saravali Census Town has total administration over 2,209 houses to which it supplies basic amenities like water and sewerage. It is also authorize to build roads within Saravali Census Town limits and impose taxes on properties coming under its jurisdiction.
